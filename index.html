Chess is Timeless

For hundreds of years, chess has been played by people and cultures around the world. CheckMate's Tutorial explains the rules, and contrary to popular belief, it is easy to learn!

The Classic Strategy Game

"Checkmate" comes from "Shah Mat," a Persian phrase that means "the King is dead."

The second book published in English was about chess.

The alternating dark and light chess board design did not appear in Europe until 1090.

The first recorded chess game occurred in the 900s.
